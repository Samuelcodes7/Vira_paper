About VIRAPAPERS

VIRAPAPERS is a leading Paper Conversion & Paper Distribution company in Nigeria. The Company is a part of the well reputed Sun Group of Companies which has a presence in West Africa since 1971.

The Company began manufacturing exercise books and trading in different types of paper in the mid-1970s.

VIRAPAPERS is today a leading supplier of several categories of high quality papers, paper exercise notebooks, paper packaging and stationary products.
